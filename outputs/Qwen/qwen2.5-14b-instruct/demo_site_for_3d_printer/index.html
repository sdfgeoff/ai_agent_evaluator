Calibration Guide

Initial Setup

  1. Install the required software for your 3D printer.
  2. Connect to your printer via USB or Wi-Fi.
  3. Calibrate the Z-axis by moving the nozzle up and down until it's perfectly aligned with the build platform.

Bed Leveling

To ensure high-quality prints, your bed must be level. Follow these steps:

  • Use a digital caliper to measure the nozzle gap at multiple points.
  • Tweak the height settings until all measurements are consistent.

Changing Filaments

The process of changing filament requires care to maintain the quality and consistency of your prints:

  1. Heat up the printer until it's fully operational.
  2. Remove any leftover filament from the nozzle.
  3. Insert new filament into the feeder.
  4. Run a small test print to ensure proper extrusion.
