Introduction

This guide is designed for users interested in learning about 3D printing, including calibration techniques and maintenance processes such as filament changes. Dive into the world of additive manufacturing with this comprehensive resource.

Calibration Guide

  • Step 1: Ensure your printer is level and that the bed temperature matches your material specifications.
  • Step 2: Measure and adjust the extruder height to be slightly above the build plate but close enough for proper adhesion of first layers.

Process for Changing Filament

To change filament, you must first ensure your printer has cooled down sufficiently. Follow these steps:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the extruder to melt any remaining filament.
  2. Step 2: Remove the old spool and insert a new one in its place.
  3. Step 3: Load the new filament into the printer until it starts coming out of the nozzle.
