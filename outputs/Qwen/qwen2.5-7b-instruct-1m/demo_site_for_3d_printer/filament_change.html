Filament Change Procedure for 3D Printers

Changing filament in your 3D printer is a common task that can be done efficiently with the right techniques. Follow these steps:

  1. Prepare a new spool of filament and ensure it's wound correctly.
  2. Allow the old filament to cool down from the hot end of your printer.
    • This can take up to 10 minutes depending on the type of filament.
  3. Disconnect the extruder and replace the filament spool. Heat up the hot end to a temperature slightly higher than your usual printing temperature.
    • This is necessary because you will be starting with a cooler filament.
  4. Feed the new filament through the nozzle until it's stable and flowing properly. This typically takes around 20-30 seconds.
