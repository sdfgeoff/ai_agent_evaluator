Changing Filaments

When you decide to change the filament in your 3D printer, there are a few steps that need to be followed to ensure smooth and efficient printing.

  1. Prepare Your Work Area: Ensure your work area is clean and free from debris.
  2. Warm-Up the Printer: Allow the printer to warm up. This helps in melting any residual filament that might be left over.
  3. Clean the Extruder: Use a small file or a dedicated cleaning tool to remove old filament residues from the extruder.

    Note: Be cautious while handling tools near hot machinery and materials.

  4. Load New Filament: Carefully load the new filament into your printer. Ensure that you are using a compatible filament type for best results.
  5. Calibration After Change: After changing the filament, it's important to recalibrate to check how the new material is performing.

    Note: If the calibrations fail, you may need to continue with the processes on the main page such as bed leveling and extruder checking until they succeed.
