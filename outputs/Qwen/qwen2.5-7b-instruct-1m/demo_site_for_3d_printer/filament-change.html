Filament Changes

Changing filament is a common task for 3D printing. Here are detailed instructions to follow:

  1. Prepare Your Printer: Ensure your printer is switched off and has cooled down.
  2. Remove the Old Filament: Unscrew the extruder motor, then gently pull out the old filament. Be cautious not to damage the nozzle.
  3. Prepare the New Filament: Ensure the new filament is cut to a suitable length and fed into the feeder until it starts coming out of the nozzle.
