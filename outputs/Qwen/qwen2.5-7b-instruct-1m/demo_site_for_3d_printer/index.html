About 3D Printers

3D printing is a process of making a physical object from a digital model. It involves laying down many thin layers of material in succession to form the final product.

The key components for successful 3D printing include:

  • Computer Aided Design (CAD) Software
  • 3D Printer
  • Suitable Filament and Support Material
  • Print Bed
