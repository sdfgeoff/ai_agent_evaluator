Calibration

Calibration is crucial for the accuracy and precision of your prints. It ensures that the printer's extruder, stepper motors, and bed are all aligned correctly.

Filament Change

To change filament smoothly:

  1. Turn off the power to your 3D printer before changing the filament.
  2. Warm up your hotend, then extrude a little bit of new filament by hand or with the retraction feature.

Process After Filament Change

After changing the filament, make sure to clean and wipe the nozzle of any old filament remnants.
