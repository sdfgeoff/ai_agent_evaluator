Calibration

Calibrating your 3D printer is crucial to ensure that the parts you print come out as expected. The process involves several steps including leveling the bed, checking the extruder, and running a test print.

  1. Leveling the Bed: Ensure the printer's build plate is levelled with the help of spirit levels or similar tools.
  2. Checking the Extruder: Verify that the extrusion rate is correct and there are no jams. Adjust the flow rate if needed.
  3. Running a Test Print: Perform a test print to confirm everything works as expected.
