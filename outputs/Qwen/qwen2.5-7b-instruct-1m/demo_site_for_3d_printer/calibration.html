Calibration for 3D Printers

Calibrating a 3D printer is crucial to ensure it prints accurately and efficiently. Below are the essential steps:

  1. Check your print head and bed levels.
  2. Tune your extruder's movement to guarantee smooth filament flow.
  3. Verify that the temperature settings are appropriate for the material being used.
  4. Ensure the first layer is adhering well on the build platform.
