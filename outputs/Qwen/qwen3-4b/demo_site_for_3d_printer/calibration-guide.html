Bed Leveling Techniques

Bed leveling ensures the print bed is parallel to the extruder nozzle. Use a 3D printer's built-in probe or manual calibration:

  1. Place the printer on a flat surface and turn it on.
  2. Use the 'level bed' function in your slicer software (e.g., Pronterface, Cura).
  3. Adjust the Z-axis height until the nozzle just touches the bed.

Nozzle Offset Calibration

The nozzle offset determines how far the extruder moves from the bed. To calibrate:

  • Print a calibration cube with known dimensions.
  • Measure the cube's size and adjust the offset in your slicer settings.
  • Repeat until prints are accurate.
