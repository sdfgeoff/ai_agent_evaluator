What is 3D Printing?

3D printing, or additive manufacturing, creates three-dimensional objects by layering material according to a digital model. Common types include FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and SLA (Stereolithography).

Key Components

  • Extruder: Melts and deposits filament.
  • Build Plate: The platform where the object is printed.
  • Printer Controller: Manages the printer's operations via a computer.
