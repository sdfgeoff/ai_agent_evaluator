Step-by-Step Filament Change Process

Follow these steps to change filament without damaging your printer:

  1. Prepare the New Filament: Load the new filament into the extruder and heat the nozzle to 190°C–230°C.
  2. Clear Old Filament: Move the nozzle slowly over the previous filament to remove residue.
  3. Check for Clogs: Use a needle or cleaning tool to clear any blockages in the extruder and tubes.
  4. Calibrate for New Material: Adjust print settings (e.g., flow rate, temperature) according to the new filament type.
  5. Test Print: Create a small test object to ensure consistent extrusion and adhesion.

Common Issues After Filament Change

If you experience under-extrusion or poor print quality, check the following:

  • Ensure the nozzle is properly heated and aligned.
  • Verify that the filament is correctly loaded and not tangled.
  • Check for any obstructions in the extruder or tubes.
