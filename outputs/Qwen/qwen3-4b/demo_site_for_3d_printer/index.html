3D Printers: Technical Guide

Calibration Essentials

Proper calibration ensures accurate prints. Key steps include:

  • Bed Leveling: Use a sheet of paper or digital leveling tool to ensure the build plate is parallel.
  • Z-axis Calibration: Measure the distance between the nozzle and bed using a feeler gauge.
  • Extruder Calibration: Adjust tension and check for under-extrusion/over-extrusion during test prints.

Post-Filament Change Procedure

After changing filament, follow these steps:

  1. Clean the Nozzle: Use isopropyl alcohol to remove residual filament and debris.
  2. Check for Clogs: Run a short test print to identify any blockages.
  3. Re-Calibrate: Ensure bed and extruder settings match the new filament type.
  4. Test Print: Create a calibration cube to verify accuracy.

For advanced troubleshooting, consult your printer's manual and community forums.
