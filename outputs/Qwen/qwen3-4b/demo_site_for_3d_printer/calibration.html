Bed Leveling Techniques

Proper bed leveling is critical for first-layer adhesion. Use the following methods:

  • 3-Point Leveling: Place three probes on the bed and adjust each corner until they touch the surface.
  • Touch Probe Method: Use a probe to measure Z-axis height at multiple points across the bed.
  • Automatic Bed Leveling (ABL): Utilize a sensor to create a mesh map of the bed surface.

Extruder Calibration

Ensure your extruder is properly calibrated for accurate print quality:

  • Measure the diameter of the nozzle using a micrometer.
  • Adjust the printer's firmware for proper gear ratio calculations.
  • Print calibration cubes to verify extrusion accuracy.
