Calibration Process for 3D Printers

Bed Leveling: Use a calibration cube to adjust the Z-axis height. Ensure the bed is parallel to the extruder head.

Extruder Settings: Calibrate the extruder temperature and flow rate for optimal filament deposition.

Nozzle Alignment: Check that the nozzle is aligned with the build plate. Use a printed test object to verify alignment.

Test Prints: After calibration, perform test prints to ensure accuracy and adjust as needed.
