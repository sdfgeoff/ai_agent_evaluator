Step-by-Step Filament Change Procedure

  1. Prepare the Printer: Ensure the printer is powered off and the bed is level.
  2. Load New Filament: Insert the new filament into the extruder and set the correct temperature in your slicing software.
  3. Clean Nozzle: Use a nozzle cleaning tool to remove residual material from the extruder.
  4. Test Print: Perform a 3D printed calibration cube to verify print quality after filament change.

Post-Filament Change Checks

  • Check for under-extrusion or over-extrusion during print test.
  • Verify that the printer's firmware is updated with latest filament change fixes.
  • Ensure all mechanical components are properly aligned after changing filament.
