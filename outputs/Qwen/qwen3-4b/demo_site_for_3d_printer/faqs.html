Common Questions About 3D Printers

Q: How do I fix under-extrusion?
A: Check the extruder temperature, flow rate, and filament diameter. Ensure the nozzle is clean and properly aligned.

Q: What should I do if my printer is not printing correctly after a filament change?
A: Perform a homing cycle, check for clogs in the extruder, and verify that the new filament is compatible with your printer.
