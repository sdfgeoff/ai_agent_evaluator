Essential Maintenance Steps for 3D Printers

Regular Cleaning: Clean the build plate, nozzle, and extruder regularly to prevent clogs.

Bed Leveling Check: Periodically check bed leveling, especially after long periods of inactivity.

Filament Storage: Store unused filament in a cool, dry place to prevent degradation.

Printer Inspection: Inspect for wear and tear on moving parts and replace components as needed.
