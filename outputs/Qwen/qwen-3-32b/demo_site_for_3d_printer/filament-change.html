Filament Change Process

Steps for Smooth Transitions

  1. Preheat nozzle to filament's melting temperature (+10°C).
  2. Unload current filament via printer's control menu.
  3. Insert new filament into extruder.
  4. Purge until new filament flows consistently (10-20cm).
  5. Check extrusion rate and adjust if necessary.

Troubleshooting

  • Bubbles in filament: Increase temperature by 5°C increments.
  • No extrusion: Check nozzle clog and perform cold pull.
  • Color mixing: Purge longer or use a nozzle cleaner.
