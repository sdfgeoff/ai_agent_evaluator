Filament Change Process

1. Prepare Printer

  • Heat nozzle to 5Â°C above melting point
  • Pause print and retract filament
  • Clean nozzle with brass brush if necessary

2. Change Filament

  1. Unload current filament completely
  2. Insert new filament at feeder mechanism
  3. Perform 10mm purge to ensure flow

3. Post-Change Procedures

  • Run temperature stability check
  • Perform test print for quality assessment
  • Record filament type and settings

Filament Storage Tips

Keep filaments dry and protected:

  • Use airtight containers with desiccant
  • Store away from direct sunlight
  • Maintain humidity below 45%
