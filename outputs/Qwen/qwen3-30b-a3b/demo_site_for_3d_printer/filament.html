Post-Filament Change Protocol

  1. Heat nozzle to 5°C above melting point
  2. Retract existing filament 10mm
  3. Insert new filament and push 30mm
  4. Purge until consistent flow (2-3 minutes)
  5. Perform 5 test extrusions at 10mm/s

Filament Storage Guidelines

  • Keep in airtight containers with desiccant
  • Store at 20-25°C, 40-60% RH
  • Use vacuum-sealed bags for long-term storage
