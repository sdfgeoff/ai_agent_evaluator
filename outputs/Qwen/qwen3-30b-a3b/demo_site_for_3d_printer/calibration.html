Printer Calibration Procedures

1. Bed Leveling

Use the built-in auto-leveling system or manual adjustment:

  • Ensure the print bed is clean and free of debris
  • Perform 5-point leveling for accuracy
  • Test with a piece of paper to check clearance

2. Extruder Calibration

Calibrate the extruder steps/mm:

  1. Measure 100mm of filament
  2. Run 100mm extrusion command
  3. Calculate actual movement and adjust E-step value

3. Nozzle Height Adjustment

Ensure proper first layer height:

  • Set nozzle at 0.1mm above bed
  • Use a business card to check consistency
  • Verify with test prints
