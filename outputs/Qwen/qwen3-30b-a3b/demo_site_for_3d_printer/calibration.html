Step-by-Step Calibration Process

1. Bed Leveling (Manual)

  1. Heat bed to 60°C (for PLA) or 110°C (for ABS)
  2. Perform 5 test prints with different Z-offsets
  3. Adjust leveling screws until consistent layer height

2. Extruder Calibration

  1. Measure 100mm of filament
  2. Heat extruder to 200°C
  3. Extrude 100mm and measure actual length
  4. Calculate new steps/mm: (measured/100) × original steps

3. PID Tuning

Use M303 command in firmware to auto-tune heaters. Ideal values:

  • Bed: P=22, I=1.2, D=120
  • Nozzle: P=40, I=1.5, D=60
