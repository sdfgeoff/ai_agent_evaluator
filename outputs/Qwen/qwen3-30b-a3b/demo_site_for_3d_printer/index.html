3D Printers: Technical Overview

Calibration Process

  • Bed Leveling: Use a sheet of paper to adjust Z-axis height. Ensure even resistance across the build plate.
  • Extruder Calibration: Measure 100mm of filament, extrude, and adjust E-steps per mm in firmware.
  • Temperature Settings: Calibrate nozzle and bed temps using test prints for optimal adhesion.

Post-Filament Change Procedures

  1. Purge old filament by heating the nozzle and manually extruding.
  2. Check for clogs using a needle or cleaning tool.
  3. Retract filament 5-10mm to prevent oozing during swaps.
  4. Resume printing after confirming proper flow and temperature stability.

Additional Tips

Regularly clean nozzles, monitor layer adhesion, and update firmware for improved performance.
