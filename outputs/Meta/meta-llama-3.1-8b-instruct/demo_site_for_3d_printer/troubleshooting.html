Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting is an essential skill for any 3D printer user. To troubleshoot issues with your printer, follow these steps:

  1. Check the print bed for debris
  2. Check the hotend and nozzle for clogs
  3. Check the extruder for misalignment
  4. Check the power supply for issues
