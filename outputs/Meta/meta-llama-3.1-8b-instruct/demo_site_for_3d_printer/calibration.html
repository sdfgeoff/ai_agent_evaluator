Calibration

Calibration is an essential step in ensuring your 3D printer produces high-quality prints. To calibrate your printer, follow these steps:

  1. Level the print bed
  2. Calibrate the extruder
  3. Calibrate the bed leveling sensors
  4. Test print a calibration cube
