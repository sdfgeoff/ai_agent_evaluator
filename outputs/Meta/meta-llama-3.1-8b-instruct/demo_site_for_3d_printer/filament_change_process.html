Filament Change Process

Changing filament is a straightforward process that requires attention to detail. To change filament, follow these steps:

  1. Melt the old filament
  2. Remove the old filament from the printer
  3. Clean the hotend and nozzle
  4. Insert the new filament
  5. Melt the new filament
