Calibration

Calibration is an essential step in ensuring your 3D printer produces high-quality prints. To calibrate your printer, follow these steps:

  1. Level the print bed
  2. Calibrate the extruder
  3. Calibrate the bed leveling sensors
  4. Test print a calibration cube

Filament Change Process

Changing filament is a straightforward process that requires attention to detail. To change filament, follow these steps:

  1. Melt the old filament
  2. Remove the old filament from the printer
  3. Clean the hotend and nozzle
  4. Insert the new filament
  5. Melt the new filament

Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting is an essential skill for any 3D printer user. To troubleshoot issues with your printer, follow these steps:

  1. Check the print bed for debris
  2. Check the hotend and nozzle for clogs
  3. Check the extruder for misalignment
  4. Check the power supply for issues
