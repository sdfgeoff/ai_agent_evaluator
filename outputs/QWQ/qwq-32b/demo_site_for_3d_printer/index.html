3D Printer Technical Guide

Calibration Guide | Filament Change Process

Calibration Guide

  • Bed Leveling: Use a piece of paper to adjust Z-axis height until it drags slightly when moved between bed and nozzle.
  • Extruder Steps: Adjust steps/mm in firmware to ensure precise filament extrusion (common issue with under/over-extrusion).
  • Nozzle Height: Maintain 0.1-0.2mm gap between nozzle and bed at highest point after leveling.
  • Material-Specific Settings: Calibrate temperature and print speed for different filaments (e.g., PLA: 190-220°C, PETG: 230-250°C).

Filament Change Process

  • Preheat: Heat extruder to new filament's melting temp +20°C.
  • Load New Filament: Gently push filament into hot nozzle while rotating the extruder gear.
  • Purge: Extrude 100-150mm of filament to clear residue from old material.
  • Clean Residue: Wipe nozzle with brass brush if stringing occurs between materials.
  • Save Settings: If switching between flexible and rigid filaments, reset retraction settings afterward.
  • Final Check: Print a small test piece to verify flow rate and adhesion.
