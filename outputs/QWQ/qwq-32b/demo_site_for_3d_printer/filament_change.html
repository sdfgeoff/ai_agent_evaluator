Procedure After Changing Filament

Steps for PLA Filament

  1. Preheat nozzle to filament temperature (PLA: 190-220°C)
  2. Unload old filament via printer menu or G10 command
  3. Load new filament by pushing through Bowden tube/nozzle
  4. Purge 10-15cm filament into waste container
  5. Check first layer adhesion after purging

Advanced Tips

  • For PETG/ABS: Increase temperature by 5-10°C when switching
  • Clean nozzle with a brass brush if backpressure occurs
  • Check filament diameter consistency (±0.05mm tolerance)
