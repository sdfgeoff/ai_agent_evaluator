Filament Change Workflow

1. Pre-Extraction Checks

  • Heat nozzle to printing temperature of the current filament.
  • Retract filament slowly to avoid breakage or grinding.
  • Inspect extruder gears for debris and clean if necessary.

2. Remove Old Filament

  1. Set temperature: e.g., 200°C for PLA, 240°C for ABS.
  2. Use extrusion menu to retract filament by 100mm at 100mm/min.
  3. Gently pull filament out by hand once retraction completes.

3. Insert New Filament

  1. Trim the filament at a 45° angle to ensure a clean tip.
  2. Feed filament until you see consistent extrusion at set temperature.
  3. Check for air gaps, stringing, or under-extrusion in the first layer.

4. Purge and Prime

Purge at least 50mm of filament to clear remnants of the previous filament. Use G-code: 


G92 E0        ; Reset extruder position
G1 E50 F100   ; Extrude 50mm of filament at 100mm/min
G92 E0        ; Reset extruder position

5. Fine-Tuning

Print a small test model (e.g., 20mm cube) to confirm optimal settings. Adjust temperature and flow rate as needed.
