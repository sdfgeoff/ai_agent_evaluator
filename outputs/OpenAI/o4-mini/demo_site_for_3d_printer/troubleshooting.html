Troubleshooting Common Issues

1. Stringing/Oozing

High stringing can result from excessive temperature or insufficient retraction.

  • Lower nozzle temperature in 5°C increments.
  • Increase retraction distance by 1-2mm.
  • Enable combing in slicer settings.

2. Under-Extrusion

Causes include clogged nozzle, incorrect E-steps, or filament diameter mismatch.

  1. Perform cold pull to clean nozzle.
  2. Recalibrate E-steps.
  3. Verify filament diameter in slicer matches actual diameter.

3. Layer Adhesion Issues

Poor adhesion between layers can weaken the part.

  • Increase print temperature by 5-10°C.
  • Slow down print speed by 10-20%.
  • Ensure proper cooling (for PLA, use 100% fan speed, for ABS, minimal cooling).

4. Warping

Warping often occurs due to uneven cooling or poor bed adhesion.

  • Use brim or raft.
  • Increase bed temperature by 5-10°C.
  • Apply adhesion aids: glue stick, hairspray, or PEI sheet.
