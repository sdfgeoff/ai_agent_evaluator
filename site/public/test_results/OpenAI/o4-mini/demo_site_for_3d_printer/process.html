Post-Filament Change Workflow

After swapping filaments, proper steps ensure print consistency and avoid nozzle clogs or extrusion issues.

1. Warmup & Purge

  1. Set nozzle temp to new filament's typical printing temperature.
  2. Extrude 50-100mm to purge old material completely.
  3. Observe color consistency; if residues appear, increase extrusion or use cleaning filament.

2. Retraction & Pressure Adjustment

  • Check retraction distance: TPU requires less (1-2mm) compared to PLA (4-6mm).
  • Adjust retraction speed: slower for flexible filaments, faster for stiff ones.
  • Minimize oozing by tuning pressure advance or input shaping if supported.

3. Print a Calibration Line

Print a single-layer line or small square perimeter to judge flow consistency, adhesion, and squish.

4. Temperature Tower

Run a temperature tower to pinpoint ideal extrusion temp for dimensional accuracy and layer adhesion.

5. Flow Rate & Speed Calibration

  1. Print a flow calibration cube and measure wall thickness; adjust extrusion multiplier.
  2. Test print speeds: slow for high detail, faster for prototypes. Ensure printer can handle accelerations.

6. Final Test Print

Complete a small functional print (benchy, calibration cube) to confirm all settings.
