Filament Management Essentials

Proper filament handling ensures reliable, high-quality prints.

1. Storage & Humidity Control

  • Semi-dry Boxes: Store filaments with silica gel packs inside airtight containers.
  • Driers & Ovens: Use filament dryers (45300C) for 4-6 hours for PLA/ABS, up to 12 for hygroscopic materials (Nylon, PVA).
  • Moisture Indicators: Visual hygrometers help monitor humidity levels (<20% RH ideal).

2. Filament Types & Characteristics

Material Print Temp Humidity Sensitivity Properties

3. Switching Filaments

  1. Heat nozzle to current filament's temp. Retract filament completely.
  2. Clean nozzle: perform cold pull with Nylon or cleaning filament.
  3. Insert new filament; extrude 20-30mm to ensure clean flow.
  4. Adjust temperature to new material's spec, run purge tower to stabilize flow.

4. Troubleshooting Moisture-Related Issues

  • Signs: bubbles, stringing, rough surface.
  • Fixes: dry filament, slow print speed, increase nozzle temp slightly.
