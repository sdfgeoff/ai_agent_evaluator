Calibration Procedures for 3D Printers

Bed Leveling: Use a sheet of paper or a caliper to adjust the Z-axis height. Ensure the bed is level across the entire printing area.

Extruder Settings: Adjust the extrusion ratio, nozzle temperature, and acceleration settings based on your printer's specifications and material type.

Other Parameters: Calibrate the print speed, layer height, and flow rate for optimal results. Regular calibration ensures accurate prints and reduces waste.
