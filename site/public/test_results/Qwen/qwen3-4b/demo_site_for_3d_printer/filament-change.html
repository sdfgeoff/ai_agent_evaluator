Steps After Changing Filament

  1. Prepare the New Filament: Ensure the filament is fully loaded and free of tangles. Use a filament cutter to trim excess material.
  2. Load into the Printer: Insert the new filament into the hopper and close the printer's door. Allow the printer to detect the filament.
  3. Adjust Settings: Update the extruder temperature, flow rate, and print speed in the printer's firmware according to the material type.
  4. Test Print: Run a test print to ensure the new filament is working correctly. Check for under-extrusion or clogging issues.
  5. Post-Change Checks: Clean the nozzle, check bed adhesion, and verify that all mechanical components are functioning properly.
