Common Issues and How to Fix Them

  • Under-extrusion: Check the extruder temperature, flow rate, and belt tension. Clean the nozzle if there's clogging.
  • Layer Adhesion Problems: Ensure the bed is level and use a raft or brim for better adhesion. Try adjusting the first layer height.
  • Nozzle Clogs: Use a needle to clear blockages. Clean the nozzle with a cleaning rod and ensure the filament is not contaminated.
  • Print Warping: Increase the bed temperature, use a raft, or apply an adhesive sheet for better adhesion.
  • Printer Jumps: Check the X/Y axis belts for tension and ensure the printer is on a stable surface.
