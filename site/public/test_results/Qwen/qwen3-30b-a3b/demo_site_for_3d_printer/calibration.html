Step-by-Step Calibration

1. Bed Leveling

Use the built-in sensors or manual adjustment to ensure proper bed leveling. Follow these steps:

  1. Heat the bed to 60Â°C
  2. Perform auto-leveling if available
  3. Check with a piece of paper for consistent resistance

2. Extruder Calibration

Measure and adjust the extrusion rate:

  • Measure 100mm of filament
  • Extrude 100mm at 100% speed
  • Measure actual length and calculate correction factor

3. Temperature Calibration

Verify nozzle and bed temperatures with a calibrated thermometer.
