Filament Changing Procedure

  1. Heat nozzle to 10Â°C above melting point
  2. Pull out remaining filament with pliers
  3. Clean the extruder gear and tube
  4. Load new filament slowly
  5. Perform a test print

Storage Recommendations

  • Use desiccant packs in sealed containers
  • Store away from direct sunlight
  • Maintain humidity below 40%

Common Issues

  • Clogged nozzles: Clean with brass brush and heat
  • Filament grinding: Check extruder tension and gear alignment
  • Overshooting: Adjust flow rate settings
