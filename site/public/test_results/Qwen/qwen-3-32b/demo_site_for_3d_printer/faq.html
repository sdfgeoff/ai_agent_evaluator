FAQ

Why is my print under-extruding?

Common causes: low temperature, clogged nozzle, or misconfigured extruder steps. Check calibration and perform a cold pull.

How do I fix bed adhesion issues?

Ensure bed is level, use adhesive spray, and confirm correct first layer height (0.1-0.15mm).

What causes warping on corners?

Insufficient bed adhesion, low ambient temperature, or over-cooling. Use a heated bed, apply glue stick, and reduce cooling fan speed.
