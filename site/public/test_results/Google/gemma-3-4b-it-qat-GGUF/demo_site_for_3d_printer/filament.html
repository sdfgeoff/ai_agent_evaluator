Filament Types

Different filaments offer unique properties.

PLA

Polylactic Acid - Easy to print, biodegradable, good for beginners.

ABS

Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene - Stronger and more heat-resistant than PLA, requires a heated bed and enclosure.

PETG

Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol - Combines the benefits of PLA and ABS, good layer adhesion and durability.
