3D Printer Hardware

Choosing the right 3D printer is crucial for successful printing. Here's a breakdown of key components:

Printer Types

  • Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM): Most common type, uses heated filament extruded through a nozzle.
  • Stereolithography (SLA) / Digital Light Processing (DLP): Uses UV light to cure liquid resin layer by layer.
  • Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) / Selective Laser Melting (SLM): Uses lasers to fuse powder materials.

Components

  • Frame: Provides structural support.
  • Heated Bed: Maintains consistent temperature for bed adhesion.
  • Extruder: Feeds filament into the nozzle.
  • Nozzle: Where the melted filament is deposited.
