Printer Calibration

Proper calibration is essential for achieving high-quality prints.

Bed Leveling

  1. Manual Bed Leveling: Use a feeler gauge to adjust the bed until it's perfectly level.
  2. Auto Bed Leveling (ABL): Sensors detect bed height and automatically adjust the nozzle.

Extruder Calibration

Ensuring consistent filament flow is vital.

  • E-Steps Calibration: Adjust the extruder motor steps per millimeter to match the actual filament feed rate.
  • Flow Rate Calibration: Fine-tune the flow rate for optimal layer adhesion.
